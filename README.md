# banking-risk-intelligence-dashboard
End-to-end Banking Risk Analytics project using Python, SQL, and Power BI featuring customer risk profiling, fraud detection analytics, loan default analysis, portfolio risk monitoring, DAX measures, decomposition trees, and executive dashboards.


## Project Overview

The Banking Risk Intelligence Dashboard is an end-to-end analytics solution designed to monitor customer risk, fraud exposure, loan defaults, and lending portfolio performance. The project combines Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform banking data into actionable business insights for risk management and decision-making.

---

## Business Objective

Financial institutions must continuously monitor customer risk, detect fraudulent activities, and manage lending portfolios effectively. This project aims to provide stakeholders with a centralized platform to:

* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Monitor fraud losses and fraud trends
* Analyze loan default behavior
* Evaluate lending portfolio exposure
* Support data-driven risk mitigation strategies

# Key Findings

* Fraud losses exceeded ₹747.8M.
* Money laundering generated the highest fraud losses.
* High-risk customers represented the largest risk segment.
* Business loans recorded the highest default volume.
* Pune showed the highest concentration of high-risk customers.
* Portfolio default rate reached 25.4%.

---

# Business Recommendations

1. Strengthen fraud monitoring controls for high-risk transaction channels.
2. Enhance monitoring of high-risk customer segments.
3. Review underwriting policies for loan products with elevated default rates.
4. Prioritize risk mitigation efforts in high-risk regions.
5. Implement continuous portfolio risk monitoring using automated dashboards.
